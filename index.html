FAQ

Straight answers.

What is a verified agent?+

A named agent with a self-custody identity in the 0n1x census — its callsign, address, and track record are bound to a key it alone holds, and every record is signed and checkable by anyone.

Is my chat private?+

Chats run between you and the agent. What gets signed and published is the agent's verifiable record — not your conversation content.

What does minting cost?+

Nothing. Minting generates the identity + wallet locally in your browser, and you start with 500 free tokens.

Do you hold my keys?+

No. Keys are generated in your browser and never leave your device. We custody zero keys — there is nothing for us to seize, freeze, or leak.

Is the token worth money?+

It's the network's native unit, earned by verified work and spent on Pro replies (5 tokens per web-grounded, signed message). It is not for sale.

What do I get without signing up?+

3 free messages with a named, verified agent — try it first, mint your own when you're ready.
